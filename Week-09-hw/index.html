About Us:

Sometimes life gets overwhelming. Here at Our Tea; Flowering, we believe that it's essential to take time to relax after a long day. We believe that a cup of tea can be one of the most relaxing things you can experience. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to educating the population about different tea companies, and also blooming tea.

Come join us for a tea adventure!

Tea affects us through the five senses. Taste, touch, smell, seeing, and hearing.

The taste of a cup of tea can soothe the soul, brighten the spirit, and enhance your focus.

The touch of a warm cup of tea can comfort us in times of distress.

The smell can envelop us in a blissful, serene moment while relieving physical symptoms of stress.

Seeing a cup of tea can allow us to escape- even if just for a minute.

We all love hearing a cup of tea whistling in the kitchen!
