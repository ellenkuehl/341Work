Kuchikamizake Ingredient Definitions

Organic Rice
The seed of Oryza sativa grown organically, i.e. without pesticides.
Rose Water
Water infused with rose petals.
Himalayan Pink Salt
Rock salt or halite from the Punjab region of Pakistan.
Baby's-breath
A genus of flowering plants from the Caryophyllaceae family.
